📆 Week In Review

July 10–18, 2026 · how it actually went down

Fri, Jul 10
Travel Day (Driving Group)

The Cranes, O'Haires & Rodells raced down to the Renaissance Mobile, AL a day early — dinner and a nightly stroll through Downtown Mobile, right into the Mobile Music Festival.

Sat, Jul 11
Wheels Down

Early check-in wins across the board. Flights landed for the Merzlickers, Brownes, Jenkins & the young adults. First pool session of the week — capped by an actual "drink a beer" moment. You had to be there.

Sun, Jul 12
Beach Day, Rain Included

Mother Nature tested the group early — Adam's tent didn't survive the first 15 minutes. Recovered with a Cornhole Tournament and Pedro's Tacos to close it out. After dinner, someone found the pickleball court — we may have lost Crane into the pickleball vortex for good if it hadn't closed at a premature 10pm.

Mon, Jul 13
Golf Day / Flora-Bama Yacht Club

Peak Vacation Rick Energy — golf in the morning, Flora-Bama Yacht Club by evening. (The VREI meter agrees.)

Tue, Jul 14
Beach Day / Cobalt

Back to the sand, then a waterfront dinner at Cobalt under the Perdido Pass Bridge.

Wed, Jul 15
Golf Day / The Gulf

Round two of golf. The O'Haires and Cranes hit up The Gulf.

Thu, Jul 16
Crab Trap, Blue Angels & Fishing

Crab Trap delivered the best Bushwackers — and the biggest bill — of the week. The Merzlickers skipped it for what they called a "romantic getaway to Pensacola" to catch the Blue Angels — we missed them at Crab Trap. JJ, Cam, Jack & Brady went fishing, meeting everyone back at Crab Trap later that night.

Fri, Jul 17
Last Beach Day

VREI never declined all week — no scuffles, no drama, just a beach day. Ike's opened early Friday, chairs and umbrellas up before anyone else hit the sand. The O'Haires squeezed in one more stop at the Crab Trap before it was over.

Friday Night Farewell
Wheels Up

The O'Haires squeezed in an extra stop at Sunset Grill — always maximizing trip value. One last sunset, a stop at the Florida state line, and a final dockside photo before the long drive home. Trip's officially in the books.

And that's how the sun set on Perdido Key 2026.
