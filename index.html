Tex-Mex, margaritas, and a patio made for a group of 25

★ 3.7/5 on Tripadvisor  ·  94% recommend on Facebook  ·  Est. 2018

Pedro's sits right on Perdido Key Drive and leans into the laid-back, lively side of Tex-Mex — tableside guacamole, a deep tequila list, and a patio that's built for groups. Reviewers consistently call out the al pastor tacos, the rice (one reviewer begged for the recipe), and a happy hour that locals describe as genuinely extensive, especially on Thursdays.

It's known as a reliable Taco Tuesday spot, and the daily food and drink specials are part of the draw — though the exact nightly lineup shifts, so it's worth a quick call or a peek at their Facebook page before locking in plans for the group.

Like most lively, popular spots, consistency can vary night to night — some visits are five-star, others land more in "kitchen was having an off night" territory. Worth going in with that expectation and an easy attitude.

Must-Try

Al Pastor Tacos · Tableside Guacamole · Chicken Fajitas · Carne Asada Burrito · Don Julio 1942 Margarita

📍 13584 Perdido Key Dr, Pensacola, FL

For a group of 25, call ahead — (850) 492-1701 — and ask what tonight's specials actually are.
