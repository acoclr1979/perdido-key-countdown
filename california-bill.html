The Most Interesting
Man at Perdido Key
"I don't always win the golf tournament. But when I do, I still make time to dance."
— California Bill
  • On the course: He doesn't chase the pin. The pin waits patiently, hoping he'll notice it.
  • On the pickleball court: His dink shot has been quietly banned in four leagues for being unfair.
  • On the dance floor: He doesn't learn the steps. The steps learn him.
  • On the wine list: He's never once had to ask what pairs well. The bottle already knew.
  • The smile: Scientists remain unable to explain it. The rest of us just enjoy it.

Stay legendary, Perdido Key.
